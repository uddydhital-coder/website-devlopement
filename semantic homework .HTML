Facts about my school


Macarthur Adventist College is a K-12 coeducational Christian school established in 1974, known for its holistic "whole child" approach, strong pastoral care, and spiritual focus.

Part of the global Adventist network, it offers a supportive community, strong pastoral care, diverse co-curriculars, and modern facilities to develop students academically, socially, and spiritually.
